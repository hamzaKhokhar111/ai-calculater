TOP RESTAURANTS IN JEDDAH

Texas Steak House

Texas Steak House is a great place to go for a delicious steak and other American comfort food. The portions are generous and the prices are reasonable. The service is also excellent. I highly recommend the Texas-sized ribeye steak, but everything on the menu is delicious. If you are looking for a casual and fun dining experience, Texas Steak House is a great choice. It is perfect for families, groups of friends, or couples.

Working Hours
Sun - Sat 11:00 - 01:00

Khayal

Khayal Restaurant is a culinary oasis in the heart of Jeddah, offering a taste of authentic Arabian cuisine in a warm and inviting setting. The restaurant's name, which means "imagination" in Arabic, is reflected in its innovative menu and creative dishes.

Working Hours
Sun - Sat 09:00 - 02:00

Palm Peach

Palm Peach Restaurant is a hidden gem, offering a delightful dining experience to all who visit. The food is consistently delicious, the service is impeccable, and the atmosphere is warm and inviting.

Working Hours
Sun - Sat 11:00 - 01:00
